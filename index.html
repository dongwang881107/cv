Biography

I am currently employed as a full-time Postdoc in School of Mathematics and S.T.Yau Center of Southeast University under the supervision of Prof. Jijun Liu. I am also holding a part-time Postdoc position in Nanjing Center of Applied Mathematics. I reveived my Ph.D from School of Science, Nanjing University of Science and Technology, where I was advised by Prof. Xiaoping Yang.

Generally I am interested in mathematical theories and methods in medical image processing. Specifically, I mainly focus on the theory of compressed sensing, deep learning, inverse problems, and their applications in medical imaging, including fast MRI, low-dose CT and low-count PET. My work has been published in top journals and conferences including MRM, MRI and ISMRM. I also have experience in developing parallel programs in CUDA/C and building medical image databases.
